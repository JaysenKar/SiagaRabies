    Welcome to Indonesia!

    Indonesia is a vast and diverse archipelago located in Southeast Asia. It is the largest island country in the world, consisting of more than 17,000 islands and home to more than 300 ethnic groups, each with its own distinct culture and traditions, with five main islands—Sumatra, Java, Kalimantan (Borneo), Sulawesi, and Papua—making up the majority of its landmass.
